What is DeFi Llama?
DeFi Llama is a platform designed to monitor and display essential metrics from various decentralized finance projects. It acts as a one-stop-shop for users seeking information on the total value locked (TVL), daily volume, and other relevant data across a range of DeFi protocols.

Tracking Total Value Locked (TVL)
One of the key features of DeFi Llama is its real-time tracking of Total Value Locked (TVL) across multiple DeFi platforms. TVL is a metric used to measure the total amount of assets locked within a protocol. DeFi Llama provides a user-friendly interface that allows enthusiasts, investors, and developers to monitor changes in TVL over time.

Daily Volume and Other Metrics
In addition to TVL, DeFi Llama provides data on daily trading volumes, token prices, and other critical metrics. This information helps users gauge the liquidity and trading activity within various DeFi projects, offering a more comprehensive view of the market dynamics.
